Research

* indicates equal contribution. † indicates equal advising. Selected papers are highlighted.

My research focuses on developing learning frameworks that enable robots to perform everyday manipulation tasks with human-level proficiency and dexterity. Toward this goal, my work spans several key areas, including multisensory robot learning, agentic robotics, world models, and optimization for learning and control.
